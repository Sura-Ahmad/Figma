MilkJo – App Brief

MilkJo is a mobile application designed to simplify the process of ordering fresh milk and dairy products.
The app connects local dairy suppliers and farms with consumers, allowing users to browse available products, select quantities,
and schedule deliveries at convenient times.

The application focuses on providing fresh, high-quality dairy products while offering a smooth and intuitive user experience.
Through a simple and user-friendly interface, customers can easily place orders, manage their purchases, track deliveries, and 
complete secure payments within the app.
In addition to shopping features, MilkJo also includes a dedicated section for milk-based recipes,
where users can explore simple recipes and ideas using dairy products such as milk, yogurt, and cheese.

Key Features:
1-Browse milk and dairy products
2-Easy product ordering through the app
3-Delivery scheduling
4-Order tracking
5-Milk-based recipes and dairy meal ideas
